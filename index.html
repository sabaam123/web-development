THE PRAGUE LINGUISTIC SCHOOL

The Prague school or Prague linguistic circle is a language and literary society.It is stared in 1926 by vilem mathesius.

Who was working in a Prague school?

Three domains were working There:
  1. Linguists
  2. Philologists
  3. Literary critics

PURPOSE OF THE PRAGUE SCHOOL

Their main focus was to standardize one language and tell about how one dialect could be different from others.
